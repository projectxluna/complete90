FTF 90 YOUTH SUMMER CAMPS 2020

About this Event

The Complete 90 & FTF Canada Have Teamed Up Once Again to Provide Players of All Levels TWO Weeks of Specialized Summer Camps in 2020.

The Experience

We create camps that feel and operate as an 1on1 private session or a 2on1 small group session. We understand the clinic will contain players of all different skill levels. So, the focus during the morning sessions is strictly on ball mastery and finishing. Why? To keep the focus on each players' individual technique. This means players will be a better version of themselves. The afternoon session will be tactical-based, developing players' understanding of the game. When should I pass? When do I shoot? In what situations do I perform a skill? This creates a smarter, wittier player. Our lunch break allows for additional learning in the form of direct coach-to-player interaction. This time is used to further break down the game. It gives players a chance to ask questions about our experiences as professionals and what it takes to get to the next level. (Hint: Pizza and ice cream for dinner doesn't help). Let’s not forget about some fun! We always make room for games towards the end of our afternoon sessions, as we want players to let loose and enjoy the beautiful game in a less structured manner.
