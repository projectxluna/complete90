2018 SOCCER CAMPS

The Complete 90 team who is lead by two former professionals and former national team players Giuliano Frano and Dylan Carreiro have put together a developmental soccer camp this summer. Players will be challenged with a technical training session in the morning followed by a tactical session in the afternoon. Our focus will be to develop players understanding of the game in order to allow decision making to be more effective in knowing if a pass, shot or skill move is the correct choice in certain situations.

Our lunch break will allow for additional learning in the form of direct coach to player interaction. This time will be used to break down games further and give players a chance to ask questions about our experiences as professionals and what it takes to get to the next level. In addition, Sonoma Heights has a shelter where we will rest in the shade or use in the case were conditions become unplayable.

Let’s not forget about some fun! We will always make room for games towards the end of our afternoon session as we want players to let loose and enjoy the game in a less serious manner. At some point in the week we will head over to the tennis courts for a fan favorite, Soccer Tennis.

SPLASH PAD! Sonoma Heights has a great splash pad which will be great for those hot summer days! Please be sure to pack a change of clothes and even a bathing suit.
