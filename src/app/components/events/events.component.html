2018 SOCCER CAMPS

THECOMPLETE 90 put together their inaugural developmental soccer camp this summer of 2018. It turned out to be such a success that we had a second week of camp immediately after. Players where challenged with a technical training session in the morning followed by a tactical session in the afternoon. Our focus was to develop players understanding of the game in order to allow decision making to be more effective. These decisions including knowing if a pass, shot or skill move is the correct choice in certain situations.

Our lunch break allowed for additional learning in the form of direct coach to player interaction. This time was used to break down games further and give players a chance to ask questions about our experiences as professionals and what it takes to get to the next level.

Let’s not forget about some fun! We always made room for games towards the end of our afternoon session as we want players to let loose and enjoy the game in a less serious manner.
