2020 SOCCER EVENTS REGISTRATION

THE COMPLETE 90 offers elite soccer camps for high-level soccer players. In 2 short years, our camps have established themselves as the GTA’s GO-TO destination for technical and tactical coaching.
We pride ourselves on developing players into well-rounded soccer players and more importantly, into genuine people.

2020 New Year, New GOALS Finishing Clinic

The Complete 90 and ROCA Technical Soccer Training ran a three-day finishing clinic to start the new year off on the right foot in 2020. Participants spent 3 hours a day improving their shooting accuracy and shooting power. In addition, we were able to create an environment where players had to make critical decisions inside the 18-yard box in order to create space and get a shot off.
